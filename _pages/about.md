I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Infectious Disease Epidemiology and Dynamics at the Institute of Tropical Medicine, Nagasaki University. My research uses mathematical modelling to generate evidence for vaccine decision-making in the space of climate-sensitive arboviral diseases, with a particular focus on chikungunya.
